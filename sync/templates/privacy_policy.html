Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What This App Does

Toggl Sync is a self-hosted application that synchronizes your Toggl Track time entries to your Google Calendar. It runs on infrastructure you control.

Data We Access

  • Toggl Track: Your time entries, projects, tags, workspaces, and organizations via the Toggl API using your API token.
  • Google Calendar: Your calendar list and calendar events via the Google Calendar API using OAuth 2.0. We request the calendar.events and calendar.readonly scopes.

How Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to create, update, and delete Google Calendar events that mirror your Toggl time entries. No data is used for any other purpose.

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your self-hosted instance in a SQLite database.
  • Google OAuth credentials (access and refresh tokens) are stored in the database to maintain your connection.
  • No data is transmitted to any third party beyond the Toggl and Google APIs required for synchronization.

Data Sharing

This application does not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. Data flows only between your self-hosted instance, Toggl Track, and Google Calendar.

Data Retention

Your data is retained in the local database for as long as you use the application. You can disconnect your Google account or delete your data at any time through the application interface.

Security

As a self-hosted application, security is managed by the operator. OAuth tokens are stored in the database and webhook payloads are verified using HMAC signatures.

Contact

This is a self-hosted application. Contact the administrator of your instance for any privacy concerns.
